This isn't just a slogan — it's an actionable methodology. In my practice:
1️⃣ Collect signals — At Temu I systematically organized 200+ buyer reviews instead of relying on gut feel; in my research project I scraped data from 30+ museums.

3️⃣ Validate & iterate — After optimizing detail pages, ratings went from 88% to 94%, using data to validate hypotheses.
Great product design starts with listening and ends with validation.
